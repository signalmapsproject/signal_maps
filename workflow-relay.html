Reading frame

This page turns the method into a working relay. The point is not consensus. The point is disciplined friction: different model strengths, explicit challenge passes, and a final record that keeps unresolved questions visible instead of forcing closure too early.

When the models converge, that matters. When they diverge, that matters too. Divergence can reveal source asymmetry, framing differences, hidden assumptions, or overconfidence. In this workflow, disagreement is part of the evidence structure.

01 What the relay is for

Modern conflict reporting is shaped by access restrictions, state routing, platform bias, language asymmetry, and model-specific blind spots. A single model can summarize what is visible. A relay is better at mapping what is visible, what is absent, where the missing signal appears instead, and what remains structurally unknowable.

This workflow exists to widen the source environment, separate claims from facts, test absence without turning silence into proof, force each model to challenge the other, and carry unresolved uncertainty forward instead of forcing closure.

This is the operational side of the Exoplanet Method. Information gaps matter, but only after the gap is classified, routed, timed, and tested against rival explanations. The method is strongest as disciplined uncertainty, not dramatic certainty.

02 The 8-step workflow
Step 01 · Analyst

Intake / Scoping

The analyst defines the case, time window, geographic scope, key claims, baseline source types, known constraints, and what would count as strong evidence for or against the leading interpretation. This keeps the models from answering different questions.

Step 02 · DeepSeek

Research Pass

DeepSeek widens the source field, maps visible source ecosystems, identifies thin or missing environments, builds a claims ledger, and surfaces proxy indicators, rival hypotheses, routing patterns, and human-impact gaps.

Step 03 · ChatGPT

Synthesis Pass

ChatGPT applies the Exoplanet Method to classify what is verified, inferred, disputed, narrative, and unresolved. The goal is not polish first. The goal is structure, category discipline, and uncertainty discipline.

Step 04 · DeepSeek

Challenge Pass

DeepSeek stress-tests the ChatGPT synthesis, flags hidden framing assumptions, challenges overreach, and adds rival readings that may be underweighted in English-dominant or Western-centered summaries.

Step 05 · ChatGPT

Challenge Pass

ChatGPT audits the DeepSeek memo for confidence inflation, bundled claims, overread silence, weak proxy logic, missing rival hypotheses, and places where source breadth is more apparent than real.

Step 06 · Shared Hinge

Reconciliation / Persistent Wobble

The record is reconciled without forcing false closure. Agreed findings, evidentiary divergences, framing divergences, and unresolved questions are separated cleanly, and persistent wobble is carried forward on purpose.

Step 07 · Final Audit

Hallucination / Overreach Audit

A final audit checks whether any name, date, number, quote, causal link, or narrative arc sounds more certain than the evidence justifies. This step protects epistemic honesty, not aesthetics.

Step 08 · Archive

Archive Formatter

The reconciled record is converted into the project’s structured outputs: Signal Ledger entries, case-page updates, timeline notes, and archive-ready summaries that preserve uncertainty instead of flattening it.

03 What each side does best

DeepSeek

DeepSeek is most useful when the work depends on widening the source ecology and resisting default routing.

  • source expansion beyond the default English web
  • pressure from Chinese, Russian, Iranian, Gulf, and non-Western information environments
  • research memos rather than polished narrative closure
  • adversarial stress-testing of synthesis drafts
  • proxy hunting across economic, logistical, media, and access signals

ChatGPT

ChatGPT is most useful when the task requires structure, classification, reconciliation, and disciplined uncertainty.

  • separating verified facts from claims, inferences, and open hypotheses
  • applying the Exoplanet Method across the whole working record
  • distinguishing evidentiary divergence from framing divergence
  • preserving uncertainty without turning the draft into mush
  • turning the record into stable ledger, case, timeline, and archive-ready formats
04 Where the human analyst intervenes

Before the relay

  • define the question tightly enough that disagreement is about evidence, not task drift
  • set the evidentiary threshold for the case at hand
  • name likely source constraints, framing traps, and your own priors
  • decide what baseline sources count as authoritative for this specific problem

During and after the relay

  • judge whether a disagreement is substantive or merely stylistic
  • choose which unresolved claims stay in wobble and which move into the main record
  • decide when more searching is necessary and when uncertainty is the honest endpoint
  • own the final call about what gets logged, published, or held back

The analyst is not a passive recipient of model output. The analyst is the hinge: setting scope, judging source quality, deciding what survives challenge, and carrying forward the questions that should remain open.

06 Core loop and support logic

Core analysis loop

The minimum closed reasoning loop is Intake, DeepSeek Research, ChatGPT Synthesis, DeepSeek Challenge, ChatGPT Challenge, Reconciliation, and Final Audit. That is the part that creates disciplined friction and produces the best current reading without erasing uncertainty.

  • shared question
  • independent model work
  • two-way challenge
  • structured reconciliation
  • final honesty check

Operational support

The Archive Formatter turns the analysis into stable project outputs. In the current workflow, that means structured handoff into the workbook, Signal Ledger, timeline, case pages, and homepage updates rather than a standalone daily template.

  • ledger consistency
  • comparability over time
  • faster daily execution
  • cleaner handoff to site pages or logs
07 How output gets archived

What the record keeps

  • confidence-graded claims
  • separate event time and signal time
  • epistemic type, routing, and rival explanations
  • proxy indicators worth carrying forward
  • persistent wobble with open, contested, and resolved items
  • a bottom line focused on the evidence structure

Why the archive matters

  • today’s uncertainty becomes tomorrow’s comparison set
  • recurring absences become patterns rather than isolated impressions
  • model disagreements become data points in their own right
  • the record stays usable even when certainty never fully arrives
